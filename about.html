ABOUT STRANGER HOOF

Our Story

We're Ari and Aarush,the creators of STRANGER HOOF. A streetwear brand built from two minds that never wanted to fit in.We started this brand because we were tired of seeing the same style everywhere.We wanted something darker,louder and stranger.Something that felt like it came from a different dimension.So we decided to make it ourselves.Stranger Hoof is our vision combined: A mix of retro horror,neon energy, and the kind of creativity that hits at 2AM when everyone else is asleep.Every design, every drop,every idea comes from the two of us building this world together. We're not a big company (yet). We're just two friends witha dream, a vision, and the drive to make something different.If your rocking STRANGER HOOF, you're part of our world now. Stay strange with us.

Our inspiration came from the show (stranger things) and we decided we should make a company and we thought of a cool animal name. We chose a horse but we decided to call our brand STRANGER HOOF because its a very cool name and we really like it.Our design is very much like stanger things but we have a nice design as you can see on our page.So come on and buy these horror related shirts,tees and hoodies.We have streetwear and caps.But unluckyly we only have stocks for men and kids to start of with.
